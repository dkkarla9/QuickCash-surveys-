Privacy Policy for QuickCash

Effective Date: January 18, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use QuickCash, we collect certain types of information to provide you with the best experience:

  • Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, and other information you provide when signing up or updating your account.
  • Survey Responses: We collect your responses to surveys conducted through the app.
  • Usage Data: We collect data about your use of the app, like your device type, IP address, and actions within the app (e.g., surveys you complete).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Survey Participation: To match you with relevant surveys and track your progress and earnings.
  • Revenue Sharing: We use your survey responses to generate revenue and share a portion of it with you as compensation.
  • Client Reporting: We share aggregate data (no personal info) with clients to help them understand trends, but never share personally identifiable data.
  • Communication: We may send you updates, promotions, and important information about your account or surveys available to you.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share any personally identifiable information with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • With Clients: We may share aggregate data (survey trends or results) with our clients, but this data will not contain any personally identifiable information.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law or if necessary to protect our rights or the safety of others.

4. Data Retention

We keep your information as long as necessary to provide the services outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you delete your account, we will retain your data only as required for legal purposes.

5. Security of Your Information

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data. However, no online service is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Access and Correct Your Information: You can update your personal information through your account settings.
  • Delete Your Account: If you no longer want to participate, you can delete your account, and your personal data will be erased from our system.
  • Opt-Out of Communications: You can opt-out of receiving promotional emails by following the unsubscribe instructions in any email.

7. Children's Privacy

QuickCash is not for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have done so, we will take steps to delete that information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The most recent version will be posted here with the updated date at the top.
